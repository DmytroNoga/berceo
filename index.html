About
BERCEO is an award-winning language Spanish language school located in the historic centre of the enchanting World Heritage city of Salamanca, the heart of Old Spain.

We are passionate about teaching Spanish and looking after our students. BERCEO offers a range of Spanish language courses in Salamanca, a unique freetime activity program and free study resources for all Spanish learners. Study in Berceo, discover our culture, learn Spanish and improve opportunities for your successful future.
